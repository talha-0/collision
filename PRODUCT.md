# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Freelancers, remote workers, startup founders, and small teams in Lahore, Pakistan. They choose a coworking space because they need a focused, professional environment outside the home. Decision is made emotionally (feel/vibe) first, then rationally (price/location). The primary contact channel is WhatsApp.

## Product Purpose

CO-LLISION is a coworking space in Lahore with a striking teal-and-black interior. The marketing site must make visitors feel the space before they visit — atmosphere, quality, and professionalism. The "Get a Quote" form and WhatsApp CTA are the primary conversion points. A client portal and admin dashboard also ship as protected surfaces for members and the owner.

## Brand Personality

Energetic, precise, real. The name "CO-LLISION" signals productive collision of ideas and people. Not a sterile office, not a noisy café — a curated environment where serious work happens.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS cream/sand landing pages
- Corporate co-working chains (WeWork-style blue/grey)
- Over-animated AI-generated pages with gradient text everywhere
- Startup hustle-culture aesthetics (orange/purple/confetti)

## Design Principles

1. **Show the space, not a concept** — real photos and real colors from the interior carry more weight than abstract illustrations or generic stock
2. **Teal as identity** — the brand hue matches the real painted walls; every decorative element should feel like it belongs in that room
3. **WhatsApp-first conversion** — in PK, WhatsApp is the primary trust signal; it must be prominent at every stage
4. **Motion that reveals, not distracts** — animations should help content land, not block it from appearing
5. **Dark and light both feel intentional** — the dark theme should feel like the actual dark interior; the light theme should feel airy and professional, not an afterthought

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. `prefers-reduced-motion` respected throughout. WhatsApp as alternative to any form-based flow.
